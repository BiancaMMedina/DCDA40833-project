Hello! My name is Bianca Medina and I'm a student at TCU studying Strategic Communications and Digital Culture & Data Analytics, and Girls Next Stop is my semester project. But honestly, it became so much more than that. Like a lot of girls I know, I'm always in a group chat trying to plan the next trip with my friends and running into the same problem: where do we even start?

That's exactly why I built this website. I wanted one place that had everything the best brunch spots, the hidden gem bars, the boutiques worth walking into, the hotels that actually look as good as the photos, and the activities that make a trip feel like more than just a vacation.

Every single rating, price, and description on this site was personally researched by me. I spent hours on TikTok, Instagram, Google, and travel blogs so you don't have to. You're welcome. 😄

Why I built this

The Mission

Planning a girls' trip should feel exciting not exhausting. But between juggling everyone's budgets, preferences, and Pinterest boards, it can quickly turn into a second job. I wanted to fix that.

🎯
Curated, Not Generic
Every recommendation was hand-picked based on real reviews, local knowledge, and social proof not just whatever ranked first on Google.
💸
Every Budget Welcome
From free hikes to five-star resorts, there's something here for the girl on a budget and the girl ready to fully splurge.
🌈
For Every Kind of Girl
Not every girls' trip looks the same some want nightlife, some want spa days, some want to hike. This site has it all covered.
✨
Actually Fun to Use
A travel guide should feel as good as the trip itself. The site is designed to be beautiful, easy to browse, and genuinely enjoyable.

The first thing I research in any city is where to eat brunch. Non-negotiable.
